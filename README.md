## Overview

This project explores the IMDb Top 1000 films to uncover what separates critically acclaimed cinema from commercially successful ones. Built as an interactive Power BI dashboard with a cinematic gold theme, it analyzes director dominance, genre trends, rating patterns, and the relationship between critical reception and box office performance.

Key finding: higher IMDb ratings do not consistently lead to higher box office revenue — critical and commercial success follow different patterns.

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- **75** films analysed | **68M** total audience votes | **10bn** total box office | **8.57** average IMDb rating
- **Box Office Kings** — Christopher Nolan and Anthony Russo lead in total revenue
- **Rating Trends Over Time** — average IMDb rating across decades from 1931 to 2020
- **Rating vs Box Office Performance** — scatter plot revealing the weak correlation between ratings and gross
- **Top Rated Genres** — Drama dominates the highest-rated genre count
- **Hall of Fame** — curated table of top-rated films with posters, directors, and IMDb scores
- Filters by Director, Released Year, Certificate, and Genre
